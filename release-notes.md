### Full Description (4000 chars max)

```text
Capital Cohort — Personalize Money Management for All

Take control of your finances with a simple, offline-first budgeting app built around the proven 50/30/20 rule:
• 50% for Needs (essentials like rent, groceries, utilities)
• 30% for Wants (dining out, hobbies, entertainment)
• 20% for Savings (emergency fund, investments, goals)

No account. No subscription. No cloud sync. Your data never leaves your device.

──────────────────────────────────────
FEATURES
──────────────────────────────────────

Smart Budgeting Rules
Choose from three presets (50/30/20, 70/20/10, 60/20/20) or define your own custom split — as long as it adds up to 100%.

Income & Expense Tracking
Set your monthly income and log every expense with a category, description, and date. Amounts are always stored precisely (no rounding errors).

Recurring Expenses
Set up templates for bills and subscriptions that repeat monthly — they materialize automatically at the start of each new month.

Spending Reports & Charts
Visual monthly breakdowns by category, average daily spend, top spending categories, and month-over-month comparisons.

Smart Notifications
Get alerted when a category goes over budget, receive daily spending reminders, weekly digests, and a month-end summary — all configurable.

Data Export & Backup
Export your data as CSV or JSON for spreadsheet analysis or safekeeping. Restore from backup at any time.

150+ World Currencies
Select from over 150 currencies with proper formatting for your locale.

Multi-Language Support
Available in English and Bengali, with more languages coming soon.

Dark Mode
Full light, dark, and auto (system) theme support.

Offline-First, Always
Everything works without an internet connection. No login, no server, no privacy compromise.

──────────────────────────────────────
PRIVACY
──────────────────────────────────────
Capital Cohort does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We have no backend, no analytics, and no ads.

──────────────────────────────────────
PERMISSIONS
──────────────────────────────────────
• Notifications — for optional budget alerts and reminders
• Location (optional) — to auto-detect your local currency and region during setup; never tracked
• Storage — for export/import of your budget data
```
